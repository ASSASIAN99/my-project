The Line of Control

The Line of Control (lac) in Ladakh, near Leh, is a vital border between India and Pakistan. Despite its strategic importance, Leh thrives as a cultural and tourist hub, known for its Buddhist monasteries and stunning landscapes like Pangong Lake. However, the region's tranquility is juxtaposed against geopolitical tensions along the lac, underscoring the delicate balance between adventure tourism and strategic significance.
